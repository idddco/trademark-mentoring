Lesson 3

Connectors

Writing is stressful. So we as writers need to make the reading experience less stressful. An easy way to do this is by using connectors. Connectors are a series of words that can be used to make your writing easier to read.

Review your writing over the last few lessons, you will see that you are stating points. Like a bouncing ball. These are good sentences. By using connectors your can improve your writing, but most importantly you can improve the reading experience for these who are incky to read your words.

Basic

And, so, also, because, but.

Intermediate

Consequently, in adition, furthermore, subsequently.

Let’s practice. Here are some prompts. Write a rapid outline. Come up with ideas, let’s focus on the examples. Pick one and develop a paragraph. Try using connectors to improve your writing.

  1. Marijuana should be legalized
  2. My peers are too brad oriented
  3. I cannot live without messaging services
  4. French absurdism dominates fiction today
