Lesson 6

Conclusion

The conclusion is easy to write if you have come up with examples.

Take the time to use connectors. Restate your examples. At the end of your examples, you can use connectors such as: finally, clearly and therefore.

Conclusion paragraph should read as follows:

  • Example 1
  • Connector, Example 2
  • Connector, Example 3
  • Connector, Main idea

The last sentence of your conclusion should be the repetition of your main idea.
