Lesson 7

Opener

I always wait till the end of writing instruction to teach students how to start their essay. If I thought this in the beginning, students waste a lot of time being creative. This is a no no.

Expositor writing, is a skilled aspect of learning. Our MC123 technique allows you to rapidly improve your writing. Quality and creativity comes with practice the same with adding vocabularies.

Be weary of writing a creative essay. That defeats the purpose of expository writing.

If you are under time constraints you want to make sure your writing is clear, concise and coherent.

Try the following opener "It is facile"., This means it is easy.

So let's look at our original prompt about Donald Trump. Look at the following:

It is facile to look at Donald Trump's bombastic manor.

Now follow with the prompt:

Donald Trump is a wonderful President.

Delineate your examples.

Let’s take the converse (if this is your view).

It is facile to look at the President’s economic record. Clearly, the President is no the best president our nation has had this century.

Now delineate your examples.

So let’s recap. We have learned to rapidly outline. Then we learned how to write our opening paragraph. Moreover, we learned how to use connectors to make our body paragraphs easier to read. Finally, we learned how to write the final paragraph.
