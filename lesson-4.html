Lesson 4

Connectors (Cont.)

Advanced connectors can help you show multiple facets to your examples.

Advanced connectors

  • Not only… but also,
  • Either… or,
  • Neither… nor

What you want to do is to write your assignment efficiently. These connectors enable you to put similar thoughts not into two paragraphs, but into one.

By utilizing connectors, you allow the reader to understand what you are trying to say.
